Bart Buddy

Codebase here

Bart Buddy was developed when one problem kept pleaguing our build team. The BART system would consistently make members late to meetings and work. The BART system provides real time transit data via GTFS stream. But many applications tracking the trains are not accurately showing the trains times and delays. Our mission was to create an IOS application using React-Native to develop a technology that could display train locations and delays in realt time. We used SocketIO to connect GTFS feed to our application and AirMaps to build the animation of the train systems.
